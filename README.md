# 📊 Sector Compass

## 🧠 AI-Powered Sector Rotation Intelligence & Investment Analytics Platform

Sector Compass is a next-generation financial intelligence platform designed to help investors, analysts, portfolio managers, and researchers understand market dynamics through AI-powered sector rotation analysis. By combining **Temporal Graph Neural Networks (TGNNs), Graph Attention Networks (GATs), Machine Learning, Explainable AI (XAI), and Predictive Analytics**, the platform uncovers hidden relationships between market sectors, forecasts future trends, and delivers actionable investment insights with transparency and confidence.

Inspired by enterprise platforms such as Bloomberg Terminal, TradingView, BlackRock Aladdin, MSCI Barra, and Refinitiv Eikon, Sector Compass transforms massive financial datasets into interactive visualizations, intelligent recommendations, and data-driven portfolio strategies.
